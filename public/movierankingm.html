The best movies of 1999

My top 5 favourite movies from 1999

Sleepy Hollow

Great story. Horror and romance.
Johnny Depp and Christina Ricci

The Sixth Sense

Psychological and scary. Great twist.
Bruce Willis and Haley Joel Osment

Galaxy Quest

Comedy and Sci-Fi combo
Alan Rickman, Sigourney Weaver and Tim Allen

Matrix

Action with Sci-Fi
Keanu Reeves, Catherine Anne Moss and Laurence Fishburne

The Mummy

It has all. Action, comedy, history, fantasy and romance.
Brendan Fraser, Rachel Weisz
